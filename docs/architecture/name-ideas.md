# Name Ideas

Brainstorm for naming the Dopamine app — a gamified, fake-shopping simulator
where you browse AI-generated listings, place fake orders, and track them for the
pure dopamine of it. No real money, no real goods, just the *feeling* of shopping.

Naming goals: evokes shopping + playful/fake + a little dopamine; easy to say;
ideally an available-ish handle and `.com`/`.app` domain (all **unverified** —
check before committing).

> Your idea — **Paper Trading Post** — is genuinely strong: "paper" = simulated
> (like *paper trading* in finance), "trading post" = a store. It frames the whole
> product in two words people already understand. It's the bar the rest should beat.

---

## Top picks

| Name | Why it works | Watch-outs |
|---|---|---|
| **Paper Trading Post** (your idea) | "Paper" = simulated (paper-trading), "trading post" = store. Instantly communicates "fake shopping." Warm, classic, ownable. | Slightly long; "trading post" reads a touch retro (could be a plus) |
| **Faux Cart** | Faux = fake, cart = shopping. Two syllables, crisp, obviously a shopping app. | "Faux" spelling/pronunciation friction for some |
| **Window** | "Window shopping" — buying nothing, all the browse. Minimal, premium, app-store-friendly. | Generic word, hard to own / SEO |
| **Pretendo** | Pretend + the playful "-o". Toy-like, fun, memorable. | Phonetically near a certain game console — trademark caution |
| **Cartwheel** | Cart + the joy/dopamine of a cartwheel. Energetic, brandable, one word. | Target has used "Cartwheel" for a coupons product — check marks |
| **Dopacart** | Dopamine + cart. Says exactly what it is. | "Dopa-" can read clinical |

## Notes toward a decision

- **Most-on-concept:** the *fake/paper* names (led by **Paper Trading Post**)
  communicate the entire premise without explanation — valuable for a weird,
  novel product.
- **Most brandable/short:** **Window**, **Treat**, **Tote**, **Cartwheel**,
  **Loot** — single real words, premium feel, but harder to own for search/domains.
- **Most self-explanatory of the gimmick:** **Faux Cart**, **Dopacart**,
  **Make-Believe Mart**.
- **Extensibility check** (we plan to add food, etc.): avoid names that hard-bake
  "retail/products" if you want food/grocery/rides later. "Trading Post," "Window,"
  "Treat," "Loot," "Haul" all stretch to other verticals fine; "Make-Believe Mart"
  and "Faux Cart" lean retail.

**Recommendation:** keep **Paper Trading Post** as the front-runner (it's the
clearest), and pressure-test it against **Window** (premium/minimal) and **Faux
Cart** (crisp/literal). Next step before locking any of these: check `.com`/`.app`
domain + app-store name + trademark availability, then sanity-check it doesn't
collide with an existing shopping/finance brand.
